# Expat metamap cluster: map literacy meets purchasing-power literacy

International relocations break down when teams argue from **different screens**. One side studies **urban mass** and **documented nuclear** siting on metamaps; another side quotes **PPP macro** lines from country cards. NationFiles already publishes both; this knowledge entry is the **editorial handshake** between them. It is **not** a hidden proprietary relocation rank.

## Metamaps: megacities and nuclear footprints

The [metamap hub](https://nationfiles.com/en/map/metamap/) stacks thematic layers on satellite baselines, including **megacity** concentration and **registry-backed** nuclear generation locations. The point is **spatial contrast**: proximity, corridors, and administrative edges appear before anyone dives into a table. That remains **publicly scoped** visualization—not a regulator’s licensing workbook, not a promise about personal safety.

## Purchasing power: PPI and the calculator

The [Purchasing Power Index](https://nationfiles.com/en/knowledge/entity/purchasing-power-index/) exposes the slow-moving macro bundle NationFiles wires into profiles; it inherits the **revision pace** of its documented upstream series. **Foreign exchange** moves faster; the site documents how **Naciro** aggregates high-refresh quotes separately from the PPP story. When households must price **rent, tuition, or commuting**, the [purchasing-power calculator](https://nationfiles.com/en/knowledge/entity/purchasing-calculator/) can illustrate scenarios **as long as** every local assumption stays visible.

## Security maps: keep the channels honest

Crime and enforcement-sensitive geography belongs to the **channel rules** spelled out for [Security Radar](https://nationfiles.com/en/knowledge/entity/security-radar/) and the [security maps hub](https://nationfiles.com/en/map/security/). This cluster does not rewrite that policy; it reminds relocation readers not to **paste** security screenshots over macro charts as if they were the same evidence type.

## Practical boundary

Immigration law, payroll tax, benefits design, and employer-of-record duties stay **outside** what NationFiles can settle on a webpage. The cluster only insists that **map reading** and **purchasing-power reading** occur **inside the same decision**, with **no silent merge** into a single headline number.
