# Neawolf Media Group

## Role in the product

Neawolf Media Group is the **publisher and operator** of **NationFiles** — the geopolitical intelligence platform with country analysis, maps, the NFSI score, and the **Naciro** analytics engine. When you read “Neawolf,” start here: it is the organisation that runs and publishes this **analysis and data pipeline** — not merely a domain name or a documentation sidebar.

## Who stands behind it — without the bullhorn

**Neawolf Media Group** is a **German sole proprietorship** based in **Aachen** (Reinhardstr. 1b). **Sven Schmidt** is the person named as responsible in the imprint. The business was registered in **mid‑2025**; the public **NationFiles** platform went live in **early 2026**. When you read “Neawolf,” you should know there is a **named address**, a **named accountable individual**, and **full mandatory disclosures** on the legal pages — not a letter-box entity and not an anonymous data venture.

## Mission in one sentence

Neawolf publishes and operates **NationFiles**: a geopolitical information and analysis platform with country dossiers, maps, the **NationFiles Stability Index (NFSI)**, and other traceable indicators. The aim is **orientation** for readers, journalists, researchers, and institutional users — not entertainment from raw feeds, but **structured, documented** assessments tied to sources and methodology pages.

## What belongs to it — explained plainly

The same product family includes the described **Naciro Engine** (the analysis and computation layer that feeds the NFSI, among other outputs), the publicly documented **LPU Architecture** as an architecture concept for dependable, low-latency inference, and the **NFSI** itself as the published 0–100 headline score. This page summarises **roles**; technical depth lives in the respective knowledge articles and on methodology and legal pages.

## Responsibility and leadership

In public materials, **Sven Schmidt** leads **technical and architectural work** on **Naciro** and **NationFiles**; as **Sven Neawolf** he is also visible in other fields (including music production). For understanding the platform, what matters is the stated combination of **formally recognised IT training** and long experience in **security‑ and error‑sensitive** environments as the backbone of development. Scientific references (including **ORCID**) and **Zenodo DOIs** are linked from the company and research sections.
