## Purpose of this Release

This repository provides the complete, citable, and peer‑review‑ready methodology corpus of the NationFiles framework[cite: 14, 17]. It consolidates all scientific, architectural, and audit‑oriented documents required to understand, verify, and reproduce the **NationFiles Stability Index (NFSI)**, the **Naciro Intelligence Engine**, and the **Large Processing Unit (LPU)** inference architecture[cite: 13, 17, 18].

The release is intended for academic peer review, public‑sector audits, reproducibility studies, and long‑term archival[cite: 17, 18].

## Included Documents (Mapping)

To ensure full traceability, the documents are numbered according to their role in the audit stack:

### 1. **1-Lead-Document.pdf**
**Algorithmic Geopolitics – Methodology of AI‑Driven Real‑Time Stability Indexing**  
*Technical Whitepaper / Primary Methodology Paper*  
Defines the full mathematical basis of the NFSI, including the three‑stage pipeline (normalisation, day‑level aggregation, weighted end composition), algebraic definitions, and integrity strategy[cite: 17].

### 2. **2-Supplementary-Material-1.pdf**
**Validation & Verification Report (VVR) – NationFiles Stability Index (NFSI)**  
Audit‑oriented documentation including all formulas, constants, data inventories, provenance, and reproducibility guarantees[cite: 18]. Document ID: VVR‑2026‑03‑07[cite: 18].

### 3. **3-Supplementary-Material-1.pdf**
**NFSI – NationFiles Stability Index Product Description**  
Operational description of the NFSI pillars (Security, Governance, Macro, Demographics), the 15-minute cycle, and the "Crash Mode" logic[cite: 18, 19].

### 4. **4-Supplementary-Material-2.pdf**
**Technical Note: The Large Processing Unit (LPU) Architecture**  
Describes the hardware-level inference architecture associated with Naciro: SRAM‑dominant topology, Tensor Streaming Processor (TSP) paradigm, and deterministic VLIW scheduling[cite: 13, 14].

### 5. **5-Supplementary-Material-2.pdf**
**Methodology and Application of AI‑Driven Geopolitical Risk Analysis**  
System‑level monograph covering the NationFiles framework architecture, JSON ontology, Naciro Engine, and the Legal Sources Registry[cite: 14, 15].

### 6. **6-Background-Benchmarking-Comparison.pdf**
**Index Comparison & Benchmarking**  
Functional comparison of the NFSI with established index families (FSI, GPI, CPI, WGI, etc.) and classification of update latencies[cite: 16].

## Technical Specifications & Integrity

- **Update Cadence:** Documented 15-minute recomputation cycle for real-time recalibration[cite: 16, 18].
- **Data Foundation:** Fusion of 115+ global metrics from verified institutions (World Bank, UN, ACLED, etc.) and proprietary signals[cite: 18, 19].
- **Inference Latency:** Published sub-50 ms inference claims for deterministic geopolitical assessments[cite: 14, 15].
- **Integrity Guarantee:** No manual overrides or black‑box corrections; all formulas correspond to the actual implementation[cite: 17, 18].
